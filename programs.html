Flag Football Clinic

A free one-day clinic built for athletes who want to learn the basics, sharpen movement skills, and build confidence in a fun setting.

  • Free registration runs May 30 through July 6
  • July 18 at Paducah Tilghman High School from 9am to 3pm
  • Open to grades 3-12 with lunch provided

High School Girls Flag Football League

A league built for athletes who want a more complete team experience, consistent competition, and a strong start to the season.

  • $100 registration opens May 30 through August 7
  • Registration includes home and away NFL team jerseys
  • Flags, equipment, and a draft party are included
