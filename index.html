Who can join?

The current lineup includes a free clinic for grades 3-12 and a high school girls league for athletes ready for structured team play.

Do players need experience?

No. Paducah Flag Football is designed to welcome first-time participants as well as athletes ready for more competition.

Basic sport-specific gear may be needed. Registration pages will outline the essentials for each program.
