Who can join?

Programs are designed for youth athletes in age-based groups. Final divisions can be listed on the registration page once confirmed.

Do players need experience?

No. Paducah Flag Football is designed to welcome beginners while still giving returning athletes a chance to keep growing.

What equipment is needed?

Each program may require basic sport-specific gear. Use the registration details page to list exactly what families need to bring.

Where are games or clinics held?

Programs are planned for Paducah-area venues. Final field and facility information should be posted as soon as locations are confirmed.
