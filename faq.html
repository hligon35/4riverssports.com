FAQ

Answers parents usually want first.

Clear answers help families feel confident and move forward with registration.

Who can join?

The current offerings include a free clinic for grades 3-12 and a high school girls league for athletes ready for team-based competition.

Do players need experience?

No. Paducah Flag Football is designed to welcome beginners while still giving returning athletes a chance to keep growing.

What equipment is needed?

Most players need simple flag football basics like shorts, a mouthguard, and the gear listed on the registration page.

Where are games or clinics held?

The clinic will be held at Paducah Tilghman High School on July 18 from 9am to 3pm.

What is the refund policy?

Refund details should be included on your registration form and repeated in parent communication so expectations stay clear.

How do I register?

Families can review the registration page and register for the free clinic between May 30 and July 6 or the high school girls league between May 30 and August 7. Questions can be sent to info@paducahflagfootball.com.
